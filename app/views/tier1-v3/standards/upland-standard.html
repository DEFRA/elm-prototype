    Manage and restore peatland habitats to maintain and enhance conditional of habitats to support characteristic species and carbon storage, reduce concentrations of fine particulate matter in the atmosphere, reduce deposition of sediment in sensitive water bodies, reduce impact of, and enhance resilience to flooding, drought and wildfires

  • Create appropriate boundary features

    Create appropriate boundary features such as hedges and dry stone walls to conserve and enhance landscape character, reduce deposition of sediment in sensitive water bodies, reduce impact of, and enhance resilience to flooding, drought and wildfires

    Read guidance for creating appropriate boundary features

    Manage scrub to maintain, restore and enhance grassland condition and associated species populations, recognising the inherent value of scrub in providing shelter/structure/food and nesting resource
