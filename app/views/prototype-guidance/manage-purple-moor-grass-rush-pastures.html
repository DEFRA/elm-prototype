Guidance

Manage purple moor grass and rush pastures

Use this guidance if you manage or wish to restore purple moor grass and rush pasture on your land. By maintaining this habitat you can help this rare grassland and the wildlife it supports survive and thrive.

Published 26 February 2019
From: Natural England

If you are interested in creating purple moor grass and rush pasture on your land refer to Guidance note: creating wet grassland.
