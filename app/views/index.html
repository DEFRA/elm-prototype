Alpha sprint 3

05 Jun to 19 Jun

End to end service storyboard

An end-to-end service storyboard, assuming that the application process will be adviser led.

Research notes

The storyboard was researched with 4 advisers in 3 remote moderated sessions.

The key finds were that:

  • advisers are best placed to deliver awareness of ELM (potential bias)
  • collaboration needs to be considered before the land audit
  • advsiers were concered about auditing, advising and inspecting
